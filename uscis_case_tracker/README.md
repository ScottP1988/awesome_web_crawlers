## Usage:
`uscis.py [13-digits case number]`

## Example:
```bash
./uscis.py XXX1234567890
Case number: XXX1234567890
Status: Case Was Received
Details: On XXX XX, 20XX, we received your Form I-765, Application for Employment Authorization , Receipt Number XXX1234567890, and sent you the receipt notice that describes how we will process your case. Please follow the instructions in the notice. If you do not receive your receipt notice by XXX XX, 20XX, please call Customer Service at 1-800-375-5283. If you move, go to www.uscis.gov/addresschange to give us your new mailing address.
```
